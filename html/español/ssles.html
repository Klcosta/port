SUEÑA SIN LIMITES
Diseño Grafico • Materiales Promocionales
Sueña Sin Limites was a series of conferences held in Madrid, Spain and Faro, Portugal by Cum Laude 21. Cum Laude 21 is an organization with more that 25 years of experience in forming Independent Amway Business Owners. They teach, through the accumulated experience of successful leaders, how to build profitable businesses in the network marketing sector.

I was asked to come on board when the prior graphic designer for Cum Laude 21 was unable to continue his work. I was asked to design the promotional materials for both conferences based on the branding guidelines that were already created. All of these were intended to be distributed through social media.
